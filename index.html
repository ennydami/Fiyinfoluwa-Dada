About Me

Hi, I’m Dada Fiyinfoluwa, a Social Media Manager and Virtual Assistant with a strong interest in content strategy, digital growth, and online brand management. I specialize in planning and organizing content for platforms like TikTok, using trend research and audience insights to create realistic and engaging strategies.

My background includes administrative support, brand ambassadorship (contributing to a 20% increase in user sign-ups for My-Lantern App), and volunteer coordination. I have hands-on experience developing content calendars, writing captions, scheduling posts with tools like Hootsuite, and structuring growth-focused strategies tailored to Nigerian and global audiences.

I’m passionate about helping creators and brands show up online with clarity, personality, and purpose while keeping processes organized and efficient. I am a B.Sc. Mass Communication student at the University of Lagos.
